**The Inverse Market Engine for Future Improvement**

## Overview

LOVE Protocol is a decentralized impact measurement system that detects relational system failures before traditional markets can price them. By coordinating human micro-interventions that prevent negative futures, LOVE mints non-transferable units of value proportional to validated future improvement.

## Key Concepts

- **Inverse Alpha**: Detecting system failures faster than markets can price them
- **Human Triangulation Consensus (HTC)**: Three-witness validation system
- **ŒîF Score**: Multi-dimensional scoring for intervention impact
- **Inverse Market Index (IMI)**: Aggregated predictive signals from LOVE mints
- **Non-Transferable Value**: LOVE represents impact, not speculation

## Protocol Layers

1. **Data Ingestion Layer**: Captures intervention inputs
2. **Validation & Consensus Layer**: Human triangulation ensures authenticity
3. **Scoring & Minting Layer**: Calculates LOVE issuance
4. **Inverse Market Index Layer**: Generates predictive signals
5. **Output Layer**: Guides stewards and enables markets
